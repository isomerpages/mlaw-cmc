<h2>Disputes Suitable for Mediation | Community Mediation Centre (CMC)</h2>

<p style="font-family:arial;">The CMC provides mediation as a dispute resolution service in Singapore.</p>

<p style="font-family:arial;">Mediation at the CMC is suitable for disputes between neighbours, family members, friends, co-workers, landlord or tenants, or any other kind of interpersonal relations.</p>

<p style="font-family:arial;">The mediation at the CMC <strong>is not suitable</strong> for legal, contractual or commercial disputes.</p>

<p style="font-family:arial;">The following is a non-exhaustive list of some of the disputes that are mediated at the CMC:</p><p style="font-family:arial;"><strong>Neighbour Disputes in Singapore:</strong></p>

<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Activities that generate unacceptable smell</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Activities that generate excessive smoke</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Animal related complaints</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Causing damage to property</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Display of unacceptable behaviour or conduct</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Dripping of water from laundry or other sources</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Littering</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Misuse or obstruction of common property such as HDB corridor obstruction</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Noise complaints (for e.g. noisy neighbours that create unacceptable noise from 10:30pm to 7:00am)</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Utterance or unacceptable words</p></li>

<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Display of unacceptable behaviour or conduct</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Interest free monetary matter based on verbal agreement for amount involving less than $5000</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Utterance of unacceptable words</p></li>

</ul><p style="font-family:arial;"><strong>Disputes between landlord and tenants or co-tenants:</strong></p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Disagreement on living arrangements</p></li>

<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Problems with rented property</p></li>
<li><p style="font-family:arial;">Problems with tenancy expenses</p></li>
